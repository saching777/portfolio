Wealth Management

Project type: Native Application

Sajag Financial Services-Sajag Securities is a well-diversified firm offering a range of financial products and services founded in 1995, a member of the National Stock Exchange and the Bombay Stock Exchange. It is the depository participant of NSDL and CDSL & a Mutual Fund Distributor. Market leader in retail broking with 31k + individual client base.

In 2019 Sajag approached to re-vamp their existing product. The Expectation was to Increase Conversion Rates, Brand Loyalty, Positive Impact on Business ROI.

My role as a UX/UI Designer was to understand the needs from business, user and market points of view and propose a solution.

Key Requirements

  • Increase Conversation Rates
  • Reduce Training Requirement
  • Brand Loyalty
  • Increase efficiency
  • Grow Business ROI
  • Increase customer base

Solution

Understood the business goals, pain points and gaps of the product from end user, administration and product owners. With the help of UX Audit, Design Principals I presented primary level, secondary level journeys, using Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, Visio.

Benefits

Rebranding benefits many ways, you get connected with a new audience, stay current, reflect new goals, Products, Offers as well as Values.
