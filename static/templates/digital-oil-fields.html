Digital Oil Fields

Project type: Native + Web Application + Hybrid Display

Using a combination of emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), the Internet of Things (IoT), augmented reality, mobile connectivity and the cloud, goal is to help operators gather, analyse and react in real-time to production-related information in the field.

My role as a UX/UI Lead was to understand the needs from business, user and market points of view and propose a solution.

Key Requirements

  • Increase Productivity
  • Increase ROI
  • Attract More and Better Customers
  • Boost the Value of a Brand
  • Streamlined Usability
  • Reduce Failuear Rate

Solution

Combination of Design Thinking + Hybrid approaches; primarily focused on streamlining the journey that should achieve the targeted goal in minimum downtime, by scheduling Product Owner, User interview, UX Audit (Heuristic evaluation). While following design principles, WCAG compliances, I created a Design System to reduce the repetitive efforts and keep uniformity throughout. All work done using MiRO to create Information Architecture, Figma to create wireframs, clickthrough prototype and Adobe Illustrator for icon creation. In addition proposed various concepts to choose right from.

Benefits

Keeping User centred approach in the product to streamline operations, the digital oil field has the potential to increase output, reduce operating costs and minimise unscheduled shutdowns of wells and equipment. As providers learn to leverage more data from all parts of their value chains captured by the Internet of Things (IoT), operators and engineers can utilise the digital oilfield to analyse in real-time their facilities and to make better decisions for optimal performance.
